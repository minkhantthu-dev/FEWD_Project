Coffee Events & Workshops

Our coffee tasting session invites you to embark on a sensory journey, where you’ll explore the unique aromas, flavors, and textures of handpicked beans from around the world—carefully prepared using professional cupping methods to highlight each origin’s true character. Whether you're a casual coffee lover or a passionate enthusiast, this immersive tasting experience is designed to deepen your appreciation of specialty coffee through guided aroma analysis, flavor comparison, and expert-led discussions on processing methods and brewing techniques.As part of our tasting workshop, guests will have the opportunity to sample a curated selection of single-origin coffees and blends.


Our brewing class offers a hands-on experience where participants will learn the art and science of coffee preparation—from understanding grind size and water temperature to mastering popular techniques like pour-over, French press, and espresso extraction—all guided by experienced baristas.Designed for beginners and enthusiasts alike, this comprehensive class walks you through the essential brewing variables that impact flavor, allowing you to experiment with different methods and discover how small adjustments can dramatically change the taste of your coffee.Throughout the session, our instructors will share insights into coffee origins, roast profiles, and bean characteristics.
